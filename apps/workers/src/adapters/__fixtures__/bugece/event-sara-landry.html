Entry Information
“High Priestess” Sara Landry returns to Istanbul presented by Jeton! One of the industry’s most exciting, energetic, and powerful artists, American DJ and producer Sara Landry invites you to the striking atmosphere of Life Park Istanbul on the evening of August 15. Sara Landry’s uncompromising, dark, driving, and industrial sound began to take shape alongside the KLUBHAUS parties she hosted in Austin. In a very short time, her distinctive style and identity captured the attention of the industry, leading her to bring her name to wider audiences through festivals such as Coachella, Monegros, and Teletech. Her industrial remix of Cardi B’s “WAP” created a notable impact on the European rave scene. Following releases on Exhale, Raw & Techno, and her own label HEKATE, her Teletech x Boiler Room set became a turning point in her career. Nearly all of her streams entered the most-watched lists and ranked among the top of her genre on Beatport and Spotify. In 2026, we will continue to see Sara Landry as a headliner at some of the world’s biggest festivals and clubs, including Coachella, Tomorrowland, EDC Las Vegas, and Ultra Miami. With Jeton’s now signature 55,000-watt sound system, alongside a custom-designed lighting and visual production created specifically for Sara, the night will be hosted by Ferhat Albayrak, founder of Jeton Records, who successfully represents the Turkish techno scene internationally with his sets and productions, and Silvio Venezia, one of the promising new-generation talents of the Berlin scene.
