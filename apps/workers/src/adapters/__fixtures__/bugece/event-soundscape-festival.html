Entry Information
Soundscape Festival returns to Istanbul for its sixth chapter. Set in the forests of Life Park on July 25–26, the two-day gathering brings together music, nature and immersive experiences across three stages. From sunlit moments through the trees to late-night journeys on the dancefloor, Soundscape moves in rhythm with its surroundings. The first lineup includes Monolink, Be Svendsen, Satori (live), Kerala Dust, Claxy (live), Julia Sandstorm, Keyrah, kitsch & SASH, pølaroit, Pandhora (live) and TWO LANES (live), with more to be announced.
